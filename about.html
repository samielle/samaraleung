A little more about me

A few years ago, a friend once described me as a Chameleon. I wasn't sure how to respond to the description. Was it good, or was it terrible? I was apprehensive but curious to know more. The friend elaborated and told me that I had a way of changing myself to mimic others from different social circles and that I was open and genuinely interested in whatever current events the group were interested in, which was why I seemed to so often "fit in". I was still trying to figure out where the friend was going with their metaphor; they then explained it wasn't because I was trying hard to copy others for some ulterior motive but because I was unconsciously doing this to make others comfortable intuitively, which produced my wide circle of associations in their eyes. I finally understood the compliment. I've been lucky enough to meet and learn from so many interesting people from various backgrounds all across the world. I pride myself on being a positive influence to others, having integrity, a happy disposition and many interests, which is my ultimate compliment to everyone I have met and mimicked - especially my friend, who calls me "Chameleon".

Having started her career in the surf industry as a fashion wristwatch designer for Billabong, Samara travelled the world as a fashion photographer and earned her stripes as an agency senior creative designer before finding her niche as a product designer for sustainability start-up Misio. Samara's intuitive personality and curiosity make her a passionate designer who interprets strategic business requirements into delightful experiences. Samara enjoys getting nose-deep in a book, exploring new cafes and restaurants, or pottering around in her garden.
